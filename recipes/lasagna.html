Ingredients

  • veg cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • mozzarella
  • parsley
  • onion
    • ketchup
  • parmesan
  • Lasagna shells
  • ground burger and brown soy protein granules
  • mustard
  • waster sauce
  • brown sugar
  • sweet pepers
  • jerk seasoing

Steps

  1. Step1: Get a large pot and place on to stove with water and salt cover the pot and wait till water boils add the pasta once the water boils
  2. Step2:Get a bowl with water and soak brown soy protein granules in for 15 min then drain
  3. Step3:Get a large pan heat it place oil and wait till it is heated add in the chop onions ,garlic and sweet pepers and let it cook
  4. Step4:Add the brown soy protien granules and the ground buger to the same pan as the chop onions ,garlic and sweet pepers let in cook
  5. Step5:In the same pan add your ketchup,jerk seasoing,mustard,spices,brown sugar, waster sauce,parsley and two tablespoons of water mix until full conbine turn off the heat
  6. Step6:Get a small plate and grater the veg cheese,mozzarella and parmesan
  7. Step7:Get a baking dish and oil it place the first layer of pasta to the pan then the meat then the cheese contine the process till the pan is full or there is no more ingredients
