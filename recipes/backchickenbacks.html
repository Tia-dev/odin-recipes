Bake stuff chicken backs recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-5 chicken backs
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • mayo
  • potatos
  • onion
    • ketchup
  • parmesan
  • milk
  • mustard
  • waster sauce
  • brown sugar
  • sweet pepers
  • jerk seasoing

Steps

  1. Step1: Get a large pot and place on to stove with water , salt and potato cover the pot and wait till potatos are cook
  2. Step2:Get a bowl and place the chicken with lemon juice and salt let it marinate for 30 min then rise
  3. Step3:Strain of the water from the cooked potatos and place them back into the pot add salt,mayo,milk,butter,spices and jerk season
  4. Step4:Get another small bowl place ketchup,mustard,waster sauce and spices mix well
  5. Step5:After rising off the marinate on the chicken add spices and jerk season
  6. Step6:Grease and oil a deep dish
  7. Step7:Place the mash potatos in the chicken and place into the dish
  9. Step8:Add the sauce ontop of the chicken along with dice sweet pepers,onions and garlic
  11. Step9: Heat an oven to 180 degrees place the pan in the oven and let the meat fully cook
  13. Step10: Take it out of the oven let it cool then serve.enjoy
